Rules

How to play

Pick a 4-digit guess with no repeated digits.

🌹 means right digit, right place. 🪷 means right digit, wrong place.

Use Try to get clues — each Try counts as a move. Use Solve to lock in the exact answer — Solve does not count as a move.

If you get 4 🌹 with Try, that Try counts as your final move.

You only get 10 tries.

Using Hint or Show Answer disqualifies the game from the leaderboard.

Example 1
Secret 1234 Guess 1567 🌹

Only the 1 is correct and already in the right spot.

Example 2
Secret 1234 Guess 4128 🪷 🪷 🌹

4 and 2 exist but are misplaced, and 1 is correct in place 3.

Example 3
Secret 1234 Guess 5678 0

None of those digits appear in the secret.

Story

The Legend of Rose & Lilly

A long time ago lived 2 girls Rose and Lilly, they were expert Scientists and they were famous for doing risky experiments. They did it for many years until one day disaster struck. As two bottles filled with Something that would explode in 600 years time broke. As years passed people kept ignoring the threat.

Until one day a person named Charlie Walker who knew it has been 599 years since the accident has happened. So he used an old map to locate it.

As he went to the coordinates he found what he was looking for a ancient jungle temple made from stone though all the walls are filled with moss it looked thousands of years old. As he looked inside the temples tunnels he found nothing.

So he walked for a bit inside the temple and found a sign it said. For millions of years the gods of water, fire, earth, and thunder have been stuck inside the old temple and the only way to get them out is to solve these riddles. He couldn't figure out who it was written by because it was covered in some kind of green waste which could be toxic.

You walk for a bit more and look down you have found the rule book!
